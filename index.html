Steph Mantis is a Brooklyn-based artist, designer, and creative consultant. Raised in her family's pizzeria, her work explores the relationships between food, object and culture. Educated at Pratt Institute and the Danish Design School, she has collaborated with numerous institutions including Pizza Brain Pizza Museum, Famous Original Pizza School, Scott's Pizza Tours, Slice Out Hunger, SVA's BioArt Lab, and Kikkerland Design. She is currently a part time lecturer at The New School and maintains a studio at the Brooklyn Navy Yard.
